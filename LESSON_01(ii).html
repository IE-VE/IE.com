LISTENING - Short answer Q

Short answer questions test your comprehension of the audio passage. They are in the form of direct 'wh' type questions as in the examples below.

Follow the instructions for each specific short answer section carefully. The instructions will specify the maximum number of words/numbers that you can write, for example, "no more than 3 words and/or a number". Write your answers directly on the question paper, at the end of the test you will be given time to transfer your answers from the question paper to the Listening Test answer sheet.

Question technique

Use the time given at the beginning of each section before the audio begins (about 40-50 seconds in total) to take note of the 'wh' question word, e.g. who, what, why, where, which or how, and to underline key topic words in the questions.

Be aware of paraphrased answers and synonyms, and also for tricks such as when the speaker may say one thing and then change their mind or correct themself.
