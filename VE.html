VE NATIVE SPEAKER INTERVIEWS

The InsideIELTS Speaking Test Preparation Program is a unique level based learning system which breaks down real IELTS interviews conducted with native speakers of English. Each level focuses on a different assessed area of the Speaking Test, allowing you to focus on and perfect each skill before progressing to the next level.

  • L1. Focus on Format and Content
  • L2. Focus on Pronunciation
  • L3. Focus on Fluency & Coherence
  • L4. Focus on Lexical Resource
  • L5. Practice Speaking Test Skills
  • L6. VE Speaking Test Interview



    • Begin by learning the basic format and content of the speaking test interview then work through the intermediate levels at your own pace before putting your new skills and knowledge into action in the final Level 6 Virtual Examiner interview.

    VE RATED INTERVIEWS

    The InsideIELTS Virtual Examiner Interview Rating Service allows you to practice a face-to-face IELTS interview and then record and submit your interview for Virtual Examiner Rating and Feedback.


    Select an interview from the 6 Virtual Examiner Interviews offered.

    Read through the interview transcript provided and repeat the interview as many times as you wish.

    Once satisfied with your Virtual Examiner Interview recording, click UPLOAD to submit your recording for rating.


    Your recording will be rated by an experienced InsideIELTS Speaking Test assessor who will grade your interview using the official (public) IELTS Speaking Assessment Rubric and score your interview under the four IELTS Speaking Test Assessment Criteria.

    VE WRITING TEST WORKSHOP

    The Inside IELTS Writing Test Workshop uses real IELTS Writing Test Task 1 and Task 2 responses written by qualified native speakers to break down, explain and demonstrate the four areas of IELTS writing assessment.


  • L1. Focus on TASK PLANNING & CONTENT (TA/TR)
  • L2. Focus on COHERENCE & COHESION (CC)
  • L3. Focus on LEXICAL RESOURCE (LR)
  • L4. Focus on GRAMMATICAL RANGE & ACCURACY (GRA)

    • Practice and understand the essay writing process step-by-step, and pick up the high-level vocabulary and skills you need to achieve the IELTS Writing assessment band score that you require. - As the saying goes, 'Practice makes Perfect '.


    Work through the practice Task 1 and Task 2 essay sets with the Virtual Examiner, before writing your own response to submit for VE rating and personal feedback.
